About BORDO

Where design, fabrication, and craftsmanship unite under one roof.

Surinder Singh
Founder

With over 16 years of experience in design and a background in property investment, Surinder Singh founded BORDO Design to merge both passions into a singular vision.

BORDO is built on the principle of creative excellence delivering bespoke, handcrafted, and high-end interiors that reflect each client's unique identity.

Every project is tailored to the client's requirements and lifestyle, whether residential or commercial ensuring spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

We believe memorable experiences come from great partnerships, where design, atmosphere, and service converge to create environments that inspire and endure.
